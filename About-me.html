Journey Into The World Of Art

It all started when I got a call from a leading yoga fitness club. The CEO wanted me to do some artwork for one of their event in Goa, and I had done some portraits and given them on the theme 'Yoga binds everything' it was a great work by jamming of all creativity. It gave me a lot of confidence and made me realize that there is creativity and interest for art in me. That was the time when I got a call from CF. They were looking for some designers. Some of my work had impressed them and I was on boarded to their team as a junior designer. This is how my journey started. CF opened the door with a plethora of opportunities in front of me. Corporates from across service industries like hospitality, entertainment, financial services etc., came up with requirements. This gave me a very big exposure to work with corporates. My creativity and artwork was recognized and appreciated by a leading international financial service organization, in their event- ArtMag. My work was highly appreciated and well received. KCP provided me yet another challenge and made me to participate in the Pan India Panorama Art Fair. I was able to make it to the wall of Fame gallery which was a big recognition. The KCP committee recognized the creativity and provided a platform to display my talents. Art to me is where we make meaning beyond language. Art consists of making a meaning through intelligent agency, eliciting an aesthetic response.
